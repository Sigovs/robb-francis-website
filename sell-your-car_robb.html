06 — THE APPROACH

Quality Over Volume. Every Time.

Robb Francis is not a dealership looking to move units. This is a selective operation focused on a specific type of car — and that changes everything about how the process feels.

When you submit a vehicle, it is evaluated by someone who knows what they are looking at. That means better conversations, more informed offers, and a process that treats your car — and your time — with the respect they deserve.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions We Often Hear.

What types of vehicles do you purchase?
The primary focus is Euro sports cars, classic exotics, classic Jaguars, classic Mercedes, and Porsche 911s. We also consider select enthusiast-performance vehicles on a case-by-case basis. If you are unsure whether your car is a fit, submit it — we will let you know honestly.
Do you only purchase vehicles in Florida?
No. While we are based in South Florida, we evaluate vehicles nationwide. The right car is worth the logistics — location is rarely a barrier for a strong fit.
How quickly will I hear back?
We aim to respond to every submission promptly. For vehicles that are a clear fit, the turnaround is typically fast. If we need more information or the car needs further consideration, we will tell you that upfront.
