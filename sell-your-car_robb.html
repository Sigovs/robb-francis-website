FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions We Often Hear.

Do you only buy vehicles in Florida?
Not necessarily. While Prestige Imports is based in South Florida, we regularly evaluate opportunities involving premium vehicles beyond the immediate local market depending on the vehicle, configuration, and overall fit.
How quickly can I receive a response?
Response times can vary based on vehicle type and submission volume, but our goal is always to review qualified premium vehicle inquiries as efficiently as possible.
Do you only buy exotics?
Exotic vehicles are a major focus, but we also consider high-end luxury and enthusiast vehicles such as AMG, BMW M, and other premium specialty inventory.
Can I still submit a vehicle if I am unsure whether it fits?
Yes. If the vehicle is premium, unique, enthusiast-oriented, or potentially a strong fit for our market, it is worth submitting for review.
Do you offer consignment as well?
In some cases, yes. While direct acquisition is the primary focus of this page, certain vehicles may be better positioned through an alternative sales path depending on condition, market timing, and pricing strategy.
