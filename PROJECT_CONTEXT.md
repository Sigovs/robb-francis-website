# PRESTIGE IMPORTS — PROJECT CONTEXT

## Project Type
Luxury automotive dealership website redesign / rebuild

## Brand Positioning
Prestige Imports should feel like a premium lifestyle / editorial luxury automotive brand,
not a standard inventory-first dealership template.

## Core Experience Goal
Blend:
- luxury editorial automotive branding
- premium lifestyle presentation
- high-conversion dealership UX

## Business Priorities
1. Brand / Prestige feel
2. Inventory visibility
3. Vehicle acquisition / Sell Your Vehicle
4. Service / Concierge / Transportation

## Homepage Direction
Homepage should prioritize:
- strong premium visual impact
- lifestyle / video-led hero
- inventory visibility
- acquisition marketing
- service / concierge positioning

## Acquisition Strategy
Primary messaging should focus on:
- direct vehicle purchase / acquisition
- "Sell Your Vehicle"
- "We Buy Luxury / Exotic Vehicles"

Consignment / brokerage should remain secondary.

## Design Direction
- Elegant
- Luxury
- Editorial
- Minimal
- High-end automotive boutique
- Premium motion / video acceptable

## Additional Differentiators
- Concierge pickup / drop-off
- Transportation by truck
- Body shop
- Warranty offerings
- Event / motorsport / lifestyle involvement

## Technical / Strategic Notes
- SEO-sensitive migration
- Redirect strategy required
- Site should support scalable SEO landing pages / mass generator pages
- Dedicated acquisition landing pages may exist separately

## Implementation Notes
Prestige site establishes design system / framework for future Lamborghini / Lotus builds.
